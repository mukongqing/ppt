# Codex workflow instructions

- For any paper-to-PPT or academic-paper presentation task, always read `.agents/skills/paper-to-presentation/SKILL.md` before planning or editing outputs.
- Preserve paper provenance for every claim, figure, table, and numeric result. Use paper filename, page number, section or paragraph when available.
- Generate and review intermediate artifacts before generating any PPTX: extracted figure index, curated text/evidence files, slide outline, slide specifications, and source map.
- Do not fabricate claims, citations, numeric results, captions, author conclusions, or figure meanings. If evidence is missing, mark it as missing and continue with a text-only or omitted item.
- Use `papers/` for input PDFs, `assets/` for extracted figures and evidence indexes, `prd/` for outlines/specifications, and `output/` for final PPTX and speaker notes.
